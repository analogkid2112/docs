# Privacy Policy for LoriKeet

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

## Introduction

LoriKeet ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Screen Content (via Accessibility Service)
- **What**: Text content visible on your screen when you use the translation feature
- **Why**: To provide translation services
- **How**: Captured only when you actively tap the floating button on text
- **Storage**: Temporarily processed in memory, not permanently stored
- **Sharing**: Only translated text is sent to Google ML Kit Translation API

### 2. Audio Data
- **What**: Audio messages or files you choose to transcribe
- **Why**: To provide speech-to-text transcription
- **How**: Processed entirely on your device using Vosk engine
- **Storage**: Not stored or transmitted; processed in memory only
- **Sharing**: Never shared with third parties

### 3. Technical Data
- **What**: Language model download requests, app crash reports (if enabled)
- **Why**: To deliver language models and improve app stability
- **Storage**: Server logs may temporarily record download requests
- **Sharing**: Not shared with third parties

## How We Use Your Information

### Translation Services
- Screen text is sent to Google ML Kit Translation API for translation
- We do not store or log the content you translate
- Google's privacy policy applies to translation requests: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Speech Recognition
- Audio transcription is performed entirely on your device
- Audio data never leaves your device
- No audio recordings are stored or transmitted

### Language Models
- Downloaded from our servers and stored locally on your device
- Your device communicates with our server only to download models
- No personal information is transmitted during downloads

## Permissions Explained

### Accessibility Service
- **Purpose**: Read text from your screen for translation
- **Scope**: Only activates when you explicitly use the floating button
- **Data Access**: Temporary access to visible text content
- **No Background Monitoring**: The service does not continuously read your screen

### Internet
- **Purpose**: Download language models and send translation requests
- **Data**: Translation text only (via Google ML Kit API)
- **No Personal Info**: We don't collect device IDs, locations, or personal data

### Storage
- **Purpose**: Save downloaded language models for offline use
- **Data**: Only language model files
- **Access**: Read/write to app-specific storage only

### System Alert Window (Overlay)
- **Purpose**: Display floating translation button and popup
- **Data**: No data collected through this permission

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### Google ML Kit
- Translation requests are processed by Google ML Kit
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We recommend reviewing their policies

### Vosk Speech Recognition
- All processing happens locally on your device
- Open-source library, no data transmission
- Project: https://alphacephei.com/vosk/

### No Analytics or Advertising
- We do not use analytics services
- We do not display advertisements
- We do not track user behavior

## Data Security

- Screen text is processed in memory only
- No permanent storage of translated content
- Audio processing is entirely local
- Language models are stored securely in app-specific directories
- No user accounts or cloud storage

## Data Retention

- **Translation Text**: Not stored
- **Audio Transcriptions**: Not stored
- **Language Models**: Stored until you delete them via the app
- **Server Logs**: Download requests may be logged for 30 days for debugging

## Children's Privacy

LoriKeet is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Disable the accessibility service at any time
- Delete downloaded language models
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- Contact us with privacy concerns
